Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, is known for its modern skyscrapers, shopping districts, and historic temples. Famous landmarks include:

  • Tokyo Tower
  • Meiji Shrine
  • Sensō-ji Temple
  • Shibuya Crossing

Kyoto

Kyoto is famous for its traditional wooden houses, geisha, and Buddhist temples. Some of the must-visit landmarks include:

  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Nijo Castle

Osaka

Osaka, Japan’s second-largest city, is known for its modern architecture, nightlife, and street food. Popular landmarks in Osaka are:

  • Osaka Castle
  • Dotonbori
  • Universal Studios Japan
  • Shinsekai
